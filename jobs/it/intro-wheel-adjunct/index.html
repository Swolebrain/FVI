Adjunct Instructor for Network+ Curriculum

Florida Vocational Institute is a fast-paced educational institution with a startup-like culture and founded by Silicon Valley Entrepreneurs. We have a passion for making careers in tech more accessible.

Currently, we are seeking a high energy adjunct instructor with a networking background who can teach a class which prepares students for the network+. As an instructor and mentor, your goal will be to help your students understand key and basic networking concepts and give them a head start into the field of IT.

Position is part time but full time hours will be available as soon as enrollment/demand allows.

Position reports to the Academic Director of the Technology programs in FVI. In addition to teaching technical classes, instructors at FVI assist with development of in-house software solutions, network maintenance and new IT solutions architecture, design of educational materials, and curriculum development. Thus, the ideal applicant has strong teaching acumen and the ability to think in a mindset of innovation and constantly striving to enhance learning outcomes through the use of technology toolsets and cutting edge teaching methods such as differentiated learning and gamification.

Qualified candidates MUST possess:

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, Mathematics, Information Systems, or Electrical Engineering, or equivalent work experience
  2. Ability to quickly learn and clearly convey networking concepts
  3. Ability to quickly pick up new abilities and technology stacks

In addition, well qualified candidates should possess a strong teaching acumen, linux skills, and a network+ certification.

Florida Vocational Institute provides a very competitive compensation package, including 401(k) plan, health and dental benefits and access to discounted training in IT infrastructure and security. We are an equal opportunity employer.
